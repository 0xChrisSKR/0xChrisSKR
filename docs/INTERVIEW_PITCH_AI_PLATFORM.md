# Interview Pitch: AI Platform Engineer

## 60-Second Pitch

I build AI platform and workflow systems that focus on the parts around the model: state, review, verification, human approval, product surface, and infrastructure boundaries. My strongest public examples are TRACE AI Platform, TRACE ProofFeed, and Immune RPC Gate. They show how I think about AI products beyond a chat box: agents need workflows, artifacts need reviewability, and infrastructure dependencies need safe failure modes. I use AI-assisted development, but I own direction, architecture, validation, and delivery quality.

## 3 Project Examples

- TRACE AI Platform: AI workflow and platform architecture showcase for shared execution state, skills, workspace, and review trails.
- TRACE ProofFeed: public demo that verifies whether an AI agent artifact changed after publication using canonical JSON, SHA-256, and Solana Devnet Memo.
- Immune RPC Gate: reliability architecture for RPC-dependent systems with Normal, Degraded, and OFF modes.

## Honest Weaknesses

- I should add more conventional automated tests and measured benchmarks to the public portfolio.
- Some platform work is represented as public-safe architecture rather than full source-code release.
- I should record shorter architecture walkthrough videos for recruiters and engineering managers.

## What I Am Learning Next

- Stronger production AI platform patterns.
- Better public benchmarks and evaluation methods.
- Deeper agent workflow testing and observability.

## Questions To Ask Interviewers

- How does your team evaluate AI workflow reliability beyond model output quality?
- Where do human review, audit logs, and workflow state live in your platform?
- What parts of the AI platform are currently hardest to operate or debug?
- How do you balance AI-assisted delivery speed with engineering review and testing?
